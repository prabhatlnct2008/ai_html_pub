Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions Answered

What should I expect during my first visit?

During your initial appointment at Northside Physio Care, our experienced physiotherapists will: - Conduct a comprehensive assessment of your condition. - Discuss your treatment goals. - Develop a personalized physiotherapy plan tailored just for you.

How are treatment costs structured?

At Northside Physio Care, we prioritize transparency. Our pricing structure is designed to be clear and upfront, ensuring you understand the costs involved. We aim to provide effective treatment options that fit your budget.

Are the treatment plans personalized?

Absolutely! Each treatment plan is uniquely crafted to meet your individual needs and recovery goals. We believe personalized care is essential for achieving optimal outcomes in physiotherapy.

How does physiotherapy help with pain management?

Physiotherapy employs evidence-based techniques to manage pain effectively, including: - Manual therapy - Tailored exercise prescriptions - Patient education Our physiotherapists will collaborate with you to alleviate pain and enhance your mobility, helping you get back to your daily activities.

What are common physiotherapy myths debunked?

Many people think physiotherapy is only for athletes or post-surgery recovery. In truth, it benefits anyone experiencing pain or mobility challenges, including families and seniors. Our team is here to dispel myths and guide you on your recovery journey.

Is insurance coverage available for physiotherapy services?

Yes, many insurance plans include physiotherapy services. We recommend checking with your provider for specifics. Our team can also assist you in navigating your benefits.
